Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2025

1. No Data Collection

All calculators on CalcHub run 100% in your browser. We do not collect, store, or transmit any data you enter into our calculators. Your financial information never leaves your device.

2. No Cookies

We do not use tracking cookies. We only use localStorage to remember your language preference. No usage tracking or analytics cookies are used.

3. Client-Side Processing

All calculator operations are performed client-side using JavaScript. No financial data is sent to our servers for processing. This means your mortgage details, loan amounts, and other sensitive information remain completely private.

4. Third-Party Services

Our website is hosted on Vercel. Vercel may collect standard web server logs (IP addresses, browser type) as part of their hosting infrastructure. We may display advertisements through Google AdSense, which uses cookies to serve relevant ads.

5. Changes

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page.

6. Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please contact us.
