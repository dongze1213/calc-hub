Free Salary Converter FAQ

How do I convert hourly to annual salary?

Multiply your hourly rate by the number of hours you work per week, then by 52 weeks. For $25/hour at 40 hours/week: $25 × 40 × 52 = $52,000/year.

What is the standard work week in the US?

The standard full-time work week in the US is 40 hours. Some professions and countries have different standards. Part-time workers should use their actual hours.

How accurate is the tax estimate?

The tax estimate is a rough calculation based on a flat percentage. Actual taxes depend on filing status, deductions, credits, and state taxes. Use IRS tax brackets for precise calculations.

What is gross vs. net salary?

Gross salary is your total earnings before any deductions (taxes, insurance, retirement contributions). Net salary (take-home pay) is what you actually receive after all deductions.
