Free Net Worth Calculator FAQ

What is net worth?

Net worth is the difference between your total assets (what you own) and total liabilities (what you owe). A positive net worth means your assets exceed your debts.

What counts as an asset?

Assets include cash, savings, investments (stocks, bonds, mutual funds), real estate equity, vehicles, retirement accounts, and valuable personal property.

What counts as a liability?

Liabilities include mortgages, auto loans, student loans, credit card balances, personal loans, medical debt, and any other money you owe.

What is a good net worth by age?

A common benchmark is: by 30, net worth = 1x annual income; by 40, 3x; by 50, 6x; by 60, 8x. These are rough guidelines, not strict rules.
