Free Tip Calculator FAQ

What is the standard tip percentage in the US?

The standard tip in the US is 15-20% of the pre-tax bill. For excellent service, 20-25% is common. Some people tip less for poor service.

Should I tip on the pre-tax or post-tax amount?

Traditionally, tips are calculated on the pre-tax amount. However, many people tip on the total including tax for simplicity.

How do I split a bill with different tip amounts?

Calculate each person's share of the bill (food + drinks), then each person tips on their own share. Our calculator handles equal splits automatically.

Do I need to tip for takeout?

Tipping for takeout is optional but appreciated. 10-15% is a common range. Some people tip nothing for self-service takeout.
